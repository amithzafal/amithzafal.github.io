Welcome to my page

Greetings! I'm Amith, a postdoctoral researcher at Brandeis University, Boston. My current research focuses on understanding the dynamics of epigenetic states at the single-cell level, a pursuit that combines molecular biology experiments with computational modeling. Modeling 3D chromatin organization to have a better mechanistic know-how of the fundamental processes driving chromatin folding and gene regulation is of great interest to me. My research interests span a wide spectrum, from cellular processes to collective behavior in active matter, in a broad sense deciphering the physics of biological systems fascinate me.
