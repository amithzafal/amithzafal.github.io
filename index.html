Welcome to my page

Hello! I'm Amith, a researcher at Brandeis University, Boston. I am currently working on understanding the dynamics of epigenetic states at a single cell level. Modelling 3D chromatin organization, active processes like loop extrusion, strand crossing and their effects on chromosome organization are of great interest to me. While I am open to different research domains, I am interested in modelling cellular processes, collective behaviour, in a broad sense to decipher the physics of biological systems.
